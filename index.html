About us

Green Beats is an experience linked to the cardiac coherence. Let us synchronize our hearts with millions of others by breathing all at once; together in sync we will raise the planet’s vibratory frequency. Green Beats was designed for Avaaz People’s Climate March on September 21st, 2014.

Simple and Effective

Green Beats is a coherence application that allows synchronization of hearts anywhere in the world. It allows each person breathing to synchronize the heartbeat . It also allows everyone to synchronize a light that beats to the rhythm of the sound and transforms the light source phone making visible the rhythm sung by both drums and synchronized hearts.
